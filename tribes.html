Q: How does this game work?

The purpose of Tribes is to reduce risk in investing in Commonwealth. Invest as a tribe!

  • When a tribe is created, each user puts ETC into the contract. The ETC can be refunded at any time.
  • Once the tribe is full, all ETC is used to buy into Commonwealth. P3C is spread equally among players.
  • Every user receives their P3C as well as a 3% bonus!
