Why should I use P3C?

  • Transparent: Control and track your savings at all times. No middlemen.
  • Liquid: Traditional savings instruments can tamper with deposits or withdrawals.
  • Accessible: The system runs 24/7 through an unchangeable smart contract.

    • The story of Ethereum Classic, the blockchain P3C uses

    How does P3C payout Dividends?

    • A 10% dividend is paid out proportionally, in ETC, to all holders each time some someone buys or sells P3C.
    • The more tokens bought or sold, the more dividends are generated. Earn from volatility.
    • Every token bought automatically increases the price of the next, each one sold decreases the price.
